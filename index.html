About Ed Jean: CEO of Summit

Ed has been involved in the Global Trade Industry since 1990. He has had a number of positions and roles from Shipping Manager to Import Manager as well as Compliance Manager in several companies.
Ed has successfully completed the Freight Brokerage EXAM and is licensed. After the 9-11 crises Ed decided to dedicate his concentration more on the global trade compliance field. Our company believes is that all industries involved in global trade should be educated on the compliance processes for both import and export.
In the busy world today many times companies overlook the importance of due diligence and proper training of management and staff to stay compliant.
We offer customized services and training tailored for your company and audits for compliance. One of our biggest solutions is the guidance we provide to put a successful trade Compliance program in place for your company.
